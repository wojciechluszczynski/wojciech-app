# Design Direction

## Desired feeling
Elegant, bridal, soft, feminine, refined, premium, calm, editorial.

## Keywords
- bridal luxury
- quiet elegance
- romantic minimalism
- old money softness
- editorial whitespace
- warm precision

## Visual direction
Use a restrained luxury aesthetic inspired by editorial wedding design and premium service brands.

## Color palette
- Ivory / porcelain background
- Warm cream surfaces
- Champagne highlights
- Blush accents
- Taupe text-muted surfaces
- Dusty rose actions or selected states
- Deep espresso text

## Typography
- Display: a refined serif for page headers and hero moments
- Body/UI: a highly legible sans-serif
- Numbers: tabular figures where financial values appear

## Layout principles
- Calm whitespace
- Strong hierarchy
- Large summary cards
- Elegant tables
- Minimal but clear charts
- Comparison views must scan in seconds

## Anti-patterns
Avoid:
- startup blue-purple gradients
- neon UI
- hard black and white contrast everywhere
- generic SaaS KPI dashboard look
- wedding clichés like floral overload or script font abuse
- bubbly rounded cards everywhere

## Benchmark mood
Blend these directions:
1. Luxury editorial wedding websites
2. Soft premium product interfaces
3. Clean planning tools with emotional warmth
