# Product Brief

## Product name
Working name: Wedding Cost Planner

## Vision
A polished wedding planning app for couples in Poland that combines elegant design with precise financial planning.

## Primary value
Help couples understand what their wedding will cost, compare scenarios, organize guests and vendors, and keep all decisions in one place.

## Main modules
1. Dashboard
2. Budget and costs
3. Venue offers and presets
4. Guest list
5. Vendors
6. Payment timeline
7. Checklist and planning calendar
8. Scenario comparison
9. Seating planning (later)
10. Envelope forecast (later or hidden beta)

## Target market
- B2C
- Couples in Poland
- Self-service
- Premium but practical

## Core outcomes
- Know total expected cost
- Avoid missing hidden costs
- Track what is already included in venue packages
- Compare costs by guest count and venue options
- Save variants and return to them later
- Keep guest logic structured by household and person

## Important constraints
- Must work well with venue offers that contain conditional pricing logic.
- Must support Polish wedding planning realities.
- Must support civil, church, concordat, or chapel-at-venue ceremony paths.
- Must feel emotionally warm but computationally rigorous.
